Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026.

AI Model Lab is an independent creator education project. We collect basic analytics such as page views, button clicks, URL parameters, referrer, browser-generated visitor ID, and checkout attribution data to understand funnel performance.

What We Collect

  • Landing page events such as pageview, Telegram click, and checkout click.
  • Campaign parameters such as utm_source, utm_campaign, and account labels when present in the URL.
  • Purchase attribution sent to the checkout provider as custom checkout fields.

Third-Party Services

The funnel may use Telegram, Lemon Squeezy, Google Sheets/Apps Script, GitHub Pages, and analytics tools. Each service has its own privacy practices.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support through Telegram or the contact page.
